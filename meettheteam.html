Meet The Team

Our People

The team Ride & Guide you’ll talk to and those behind the scenes – are united by their passion for exploring the world and their belief in the power of curating unforgettable trips that help make it a better place. The company provides reliable, experienced, and knowledgeable tour guides to provide a unique and fun-loving experience of the local culture across the globe. We also provide an eco-friendly mode of transportation for exploring the local destinations.

Recognizing this opportunity to shake things up, Ride & Guide was founded in 2020 by a small, experienced team with the support of the community. Operating on two leading principles, transparency and purpose, Ride & Guide creates unforgettable trips to inspiring places while directing more tourism revenue to the people and communities protecting our increasingly fragile natural world. In doing so, we hope to send a ripple of change throughout the industry. This is just the start.
